Report Access & Usage Guidelines

Report Frequency Types:

  • Daily: Critical operational reports requiring daily monitoring
  • Weekly: Action-required reports and exception management
  • Monthly: Standard operational and compliance reporting
  • Quarterly: Financial, tax, and quality assurance reviews
  • Annual: Year-end reporting and statistical analysis
  • Ad-Hoc: On-demand reports run as needed

Report Access Levels:

  • County Level: County-specific data accessible to county staff
  • State Level: Statewide data accessible to state oversight
  • State & County: Available at both levels with appropriate filters
